Why we do what we do

Facing global climate change, economic uncertainties, a growing population, and more people moving to urban areas, technology is required to ensure our food supplies. Precision Ag is a proven approach, but there are barriers to access: cost, complexity, vendor lock, etc. The mission of PAGAF is to make Precision Agriculture capabilities available to any farmer who wishes to use it, at no cost.

PAGAF is Open-Source Technology...

...And is powered by Open-Source Technology

PAGAF is built:

  • To leverage as many free US Government data investments as heavily as possible
  • To adhere to NIST 800-53 security standards to ensure the integrity of all data stored within the system
  • To take advantage of cloud-first and modern computing design patterns to run at the lowest costs and scale to any size need
  • On open-source software
  • To be open-source software
  • To be 100% web/browser based
  • To expose all capabilities via APIs
